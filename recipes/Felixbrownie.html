Felix Brownies

This is the personal brownie recipe of K pop idol, Felix, of Stray Kids! Said to be the most delicious brownies of the world, Felix often gifts these scrumptious treats to friends, family, staff, and fellow idols. You may never get to meet Felix in person, but with this recipe, you can feel close to this handsome K pop idol and pretend that he made them for you himself. This is not your typical brownie recipe. Felix's unique concoction consists of his own special blend of ingredients including espresso.

Ingredients

  • 115g melted unsalted butter
  • 30g brown sugar
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp espresso
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g all purpose flour
  • 20g bread flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
    • pinch of salt
  • 150g chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Mix bowl 2 ingredients together then add them to bowl 1.
  3. Gently fold together and then fold in the chocolate.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Enjoy the "TASTE" of Felix!
